About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Jishuang Chen. I am a graduate student majoring in computer science at the University of Southern California. I completed my undergraduate studies at Sichuan University in China, where I was enrolled in the top 1% of the Computer Science honors program.

During my undergraduate studies, I gained a strong foundation in computer science fundamentals and programming skills, which provided me with a solid base for my future studies and work experience. I also had the opportunity to participate in the Computer Graphics Laboratory at Sichuan University, where I developed expertise in 3D graphics, industry graphic design software engine design. In this lab, I worked on several projects related to computer graphics, including the development of a basic C++ library for a big indystry graphic design software.

After completing my undergraduate studies, I continued my education at the University of Southern California, where I am currently pursuing a master's degree in computer science with a focus on game development, web development, and database design and management.

In terms of game development, I have participated in several game development projects as a core programmer and team leader, and have developed multiple games that received positive reviews. In one of the projects, I led the development team to participate in the Global Game Jam (GGJ), a global game development competition. As the core programmer of the team, I used the Unity engine to complete game level design, character AI, and other related development tasks, and coordinated team members to complete other aspects of the project.

In addition to game development, I am also proficient in web development, with experience in independently developing web applications. I am proficient in several programming languages, such as Python, Java, and JavaScript, and am able to use programming frameworks and tools like Django, React, and Vue for web development. Furthermore, I have strong skills in database design and management, and am able to use database technologies such as MySQL and MongoDB for data storage and querying.

Thank you for taking the time to learn about my background. If you are interested in my skills and work experience, please feel free to contact me at any time.
